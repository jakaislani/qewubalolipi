What was Richard Buckley’s net worth?

Richard Buckley, a renowned American writer and journalist, had a net worth of $50 million at the time of his death in September 2021 at the age of 72. He was famously known for being married to the fashion designer, film director, producer, and screenwriter Tom Ford, with whom he had been partners for over three decades.

The couple first met at a fashion show in 1986, and Tom later revealed that he knew Richard was the one by the end of their elevator ride, saying, “It was literally love at first sight.” They welcomed a son together in 2012 and were legally married in 2014.

Richard began his career as a fashion journalist at New York Magazine in 1979 and went on to write and edit for various magazines, including Italian Vogue, Dutch, Vanity Fair, Mirabella, W, and WWD. He also served as the editor in chief for Vogue Hommes International for a period of time.

In 2009, Richard had an uncredited role in Tom Ford’s movie “A Single Man.” The couple owned multiple properties in Los Angeles, London, New York, and Santa Fe, with a combined worth of over $100 million.
