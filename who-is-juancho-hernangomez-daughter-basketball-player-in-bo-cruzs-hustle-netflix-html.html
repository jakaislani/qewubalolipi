Juancho Hernangómez, born on September 28, 1995, is a Spanish basketball ace currently showcasing his skills with Panathinaikos in the Greek Basket League and the EuroLeague. But before his European stints, he made his mark in the NBA, starting with the Denver Nuggets, who drafted him as the 15th pick in 2016.

The Basketball Dynasty: Family Roots

The Hernangómez family breathes basketball. His older brother, Willy Hernangómez, is with the New Orleans Pelicans after stints with Real Madrid. And get this, their mom, Margarita Geuer Draeger, led Spain to a European title in ’93! Even their dad played for Real Madrid and Estudiantes. Talk about a powerhouse family, right?

Hoops Journey

Juancho’s basketball journey kicked off in 2007, and boy, has he made moves since then! From youth teams at CB Las Rozas to Real Madrid, and later, CB Estudiantes, he’s showcased his skills at every step. The Denver Nuggets drafted him in 2016, and he made quite the splash, contributing to record-setting games and catching everyone’s eye.

NBA and International Stints

His NBA journey took him through teams like the Timberwolves, Celtics, Spurs, and Jazz. On the international stage, he’s represented Spain at various tournaments, bagging bronze at EuroBasket 2017 and securing a spot in the 2019 FIBA World Cup.

Off-Court Glimpse

Off the court, Juancho’s all about his career. At 26, he’s not into the marriage scene or parenting just yet. But in his latest movie, “Hustle,” he plays a dad named Lucia’s dad, co-parenting with the mom—though the actress playing his wife remains a mystery. The Dollars and Cents

Money talk! His net worth? A cool $17 million! He’s earned it through his basketball prowess, scoring hefty contracts, like the $21 million, three-year deal with the Timberwolves.

Wrap-Up

Juancho Hernangómez is not just a basketball wizard; he’s a rising star whose journey reflects dedication and skill. His family’s legacy fuels his passion, and his on-court finesse speaks volumes. While off the court, he’s all about personal growth and career goals.
