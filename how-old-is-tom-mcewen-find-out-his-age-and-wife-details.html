How Old Is Tom McEwen? Find Out His Age And Wife Details

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Tom McEwen of age 30 has been riding for years now having come from an equestrian family. He recently has won a gold medal for the Great Britain team in the Tokyo Olympics and remained ahead in the game.

Tom is an equestrian who usually competes in eventing.

He won a gold in 2010, riding Major Sweep, and then went on to win another gold with his team in 2011, where he rode Private Rudolph. The most notable horse he has rode is Toledo de Kerser.

How Old is Tom McEwen? His Age

Tom McEwen’s age is 30 years old.

He was born on May 10, 1991, and his zodiac sign is Taurus.

Tom had an interest in horses from his early childhood as his parents, Bobby and Ali McEwen, were both equestrians. His mother, Ali, was a familiar face on the showjumping circuit, and his father is a Clinical Director at the Valley Equine Hospital Lambourn.

The Olympian has a sister named Ella, who is also involved in events and was a part of the British Pony Team in 2007 and 2008.
