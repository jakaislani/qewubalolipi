Chapter 114 Spoilers

Chapter 114 follows Ha Do-wan’s quest to find Anne Bonny and her ship. But his journey is full of challenges, like facing scary sea creatures and dangerous storms. There’s a new twist where he fights other pirates using playing cards given by Blackbeard, each card having a weapon and a parrot friend.

We also learn more about Anne Bonny’s past and why she took Ha Do-wan’s card. Her actions raise questions about how involved she is in the quest and if she lets others do the work.

Summary of Chapter 113

In the previous chapter, Ha Do-wan played the game non-stop for seven days to impress a girl at school. But this made him very tired and worn out. Surprisingly, he then gets sent back in time one week before he reached his limit. Even though he’s back in class, he still has powers from the game. Now, he’s ready to change his life and confront those who troubled him.
