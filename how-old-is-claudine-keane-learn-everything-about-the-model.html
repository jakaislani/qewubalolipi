How Old Is Claudine Keane?

Claudine is 38 years old as of 2021. She was born in 1983 in Dublin, Ireland.

At the age of 21, she was a Miss Ireland contestant in 2004. She worked as a model and had an impressive modelling career.

When she was just 18 years old, she met the love of her life. At that time, Robbie was beginning his career as a footballer.

Claudine graduated with an economics and finance degree from the University College Dublin. Moreover, she has worked as a newspaper columnist.

Is Claudine Keane Married? Family Details Explored

Yes, Claudine is married to the Irish legend, Robbie Keane. The pair first met through mutual friends.

Robbie and Claudine married in June 2008. They tied the knot at St Alphonsus and Columbia Church in Ballybrack, Dublin. They have two sons.

Claudine and Robbie gave birth to Robert in 2009 and Hudson in 2015. Her eldest son, Robert Ronan, was named after his father and grandfather.

Spending five years in Los Angeles during Robbie’s time in LA Galaxy, the family lives happily in a lavish Dublin mansion.
