Introduction

Rod Wave is an American hip-hop star that achieved his start in music with his viral hit “Heart on Ice.”

In a relatively short space of time, he has emerged as one of the most popular up-and-coming names in hip hop and has even topped the Billboard 200 charts.

Below, we explore Rod Wave’s net worth and explain how this rap star has made his money. 

The Origin Story

Rod Wave – real name Rodarius Marcell Green – was born on August 27, 1998, in St Petersburg, Florida.

He had a relatively tough time growing up, overcoming some family issues to make it to the top. 

His parents divorced when he was in elementary school, and his dad ended up in prison.

This led Rod to look to the streets for money as his life at home became more and more difficult. 

He spent several years involved in criminal activity and was in and out of juvenile detention centers.

One of his arrests saw him detained for carrying a weapon at his school.

When his father was released from prison, he decided to support Rod to achieve his dream as a musician and even bought him his first microphone to show his support.

Rod Wave – Net Worth

So, how much is Rod Wave worth?

As a big name in American hip-hop and R&B, Rod Wave has earned most of his money from his musical endeavors.

Widely known for his contribution to the sub-genre soul-trap, he made his name in 2019 when his single “Heart on Ice” went viral. 

It eventually peaked at number 25 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and was viewed millions of times on YouTube and TikTok in the months that followed.

YouTube

Wave has more than 4.9 million subscribers on YouTube, and he has enjoyed incredible success since starting his account in 2016. 

His videos have been viewed more than 4.1 billion times, which has brought him a huge amount of ad revenue alone. One billion views on YouTube equates to approximately $3 million, so we can reasonably say that Wave has earned at least $12 million (before taxes) from YouTube ads alone. 

His videos regularly receive tens of millions of views. For instance, the official video for “Heart on Ice” has racked up more than 173 million views, and it’s undoubtedly the track that put Rod Wave on the map.

Other popular releases for the musician include “Letter from Houston” and “Girl of my Dreams,” racking up 89 million and 72 million views, respectively. 

When Wave released Ghetto Gospel in 2019, he couldn’t possibly have imagined the incredible success it would enjoy. It peaked at number 10 on the Billboard 200, and he followed it up with Pray 4 Love, which sat at number 2 on the charts the following year.

This wasn’t enough for Wave, and his third album, Soulfly, debuted at number 1 on the Billboard 200, securing his first chart-topping album at only the third attempt.

The Florida-born rapper might be relatively new to the scene, but his incredibly fast rise to the top has seen him make a name for himself in hip-hop already. 

Although he became popular as a result of a viral hit, Rod Wave is now involved in various rap labels, including Alamo, Geffen, Interscope, and Hit House.

He also regularly collaborates with other artists, including Kevin Gates, which has helped him build his profile as a rap artist. 

As a direct result of the success of his first three albums, Rod Wave has enjoyed huge financial success off the back of his music.

He racks up millions of streams on the likes of Spotify and Apple Music, and his star will continue shining bright when his next studio album drops. 

Another source of revenue for the popular hip-hop star is touring, and he regularly plays sold-out venues at different locations.

For such a relatively new act, it’s hard to think of Rod Wave’s story and not be impressed.

He has received praise from various sources in the music industry for his talent. 

On Instagram, Wave has around 6.5 million followers, and he keeps them updated with the goings-on in his career. It’s not clear if Wave makes money through brand endorsements on the platform, but given his reputation, it’s no doubt another potential revenue stream for the star. 

As a result of his incredible success since going viral with “Heart on Ice,” Rod Wave has become a much-loved mainstream musician in no time at all.

Therefore, American hip-hop star Rod Wave has an estimated net worth of $8 million.
