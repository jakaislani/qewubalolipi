Jonathan Cohen – Biography

Jonathan Cohen is a gifted and well-respected French actor. After portraying Serge le Mytho in Bloques, this young actor came to prominence. After his performance of Charles in Bref, he became well-known. Jonathan is well-liked by his audience, who eagerly anticipate his film appearance. He was born on June 16, 1980, in Pantin, Seine-Saint-Denis, France, and is of French nationality. Pantin is a commune in the northeastern suburbs of Paris, France, and is one of the most densely populated areas in the city.

He began his business selling windows as a real estate agent. During that time, a friend recommended that he go to a play, which helped him find his love for acting. Jonathan left his job two months later and enrolled in a theatrical school to improve his acting skills. Jonathan and his friends relocated to New York to pursue a career. He started working there as a server, but they had to return to Paris following the dreadful and horrifying September 11 attacks.

Jonathan Cohen- Professional Career

Jonathan had his first appearance on screen in the popular comedy Comme t’y es belle in the year 2006. As a result, he was able to obtain a few tiny roles in both cinema and television. Soon after, he landed his big break as the lead in the two-season series Les Invincibles, which aired on the Arte channel. Hassan was his character. In 2011, the young actor became a well-known face on the Canal + channel thanks to his outstanding performance. His performance was well-received by the audience. Les 2 mecs qui travaillent a Canal, Bref, A Perfect Plan, Amour et Turbulence, and others are among his major works.

He was also heavily involved in television, producing a few programs that were well-received by his fan base. He appeared in some notable films, including Blocked, Serge le Mytho, Hero Corp, France Kbek, Supercondrianque, the long-awaited comedy, La Crème de la Crème, and many others. Jonathan developed into a well-known and excellent actor with a good sense of humor in his parts, leading to leading roles in films such as Us three or nothing, Vicky, Bloques, Papa ou Maman 2, De plus belle, and Budapest. In 2019, he was a part of the web series Family Business. In the year 2019, Netflix released the first season. This year, in 2020, the second season will be released. Igor Gotesman directed the French web series, which follows the narrative of a failing entrepreneur.
