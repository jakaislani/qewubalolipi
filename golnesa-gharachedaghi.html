Meet Golnesa Gharachedaghi Aka GG, Plastic Surgery, Husband, Bio &amp; Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 3, 2024

Golnesa Gharachedaghi, widely known as GG, is an Iranian-American entrepreneur and a television model. She earned international fame by starring in the American reality TV series Shahs of Sunset.

Moreover, Golnesa is a mother as well as a successful businesswoman who owns and handles multiple businesses. She is the CEO of Wüsah, a medicinal cannabis company.

Relationship: Is Golnesa Gharachedaghi Single?

Throughout the period of time in Bravo's Shahs of Sunset, we have seen GG going through her personal moments like dating and marriage.

She was engaged to Shalom Yeroushalmi in December 2016 after meeting him in the apartment complex and dating for a short time. The ex-couple finally decided to tie their knot in January 2017 by organizing a surprise wedding for their fans and family.

However, just after seven weeks of their marriage, in March 2017, GG and Shalom got separated and decided to end their marriage. Golnesa filed for divorce in May 2017, declaring irreconcilable differences. Nearly two years after she filed the divorce, they officially divorced in January 2019.

At this date, the Shahs of Sunset star Golnesa is single and happy to live her life this way. She claims that being independent and single is far happier than being in a relationship.

Reason For Golnesa Quick Divorce

"Shalom Yeroushalmi was more traditional than I am," GG claimed. She said that she never believed in marriage. The only reason she married Shalom was to make him happy, she also quoted:

"I knew what I did was wrong and it wasn't for me or for my happiness, but for someone else, and I guess for one moment I thought maybe someone else's happiness will do it for me. And it didn't."

The fan favorite of Shahs of Sunset also added that marrying Shalom was her biggest mistake, and she will try hard not to regret that decision. On Watch What Happens Live, she claimed that all Shalom wanted was her money, and he decided to go for it legally. 

Children: Does GG Have Any Children?

Despite being single, GG has a son Elijah Javad Gharachedaghi. She frequently posts her son's photos on her social media. The baby boy was born on May 2, 2020.

The aspiring entrepreneur said that she always wanted a child, and it was unnecessary for her to have a life partner. Therefore she took the help of a sperm donor to begin her motherhood.

After going through the final round of In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF) in August 2019, she announced in October 2019 that she was expecting her first child.

Is Golnesa Gharachedagahi "GG" Rich?

Being a successful entrepreneur and a reality TV star, Golnesa Gharachedaghi has an estimated net worth of $14 million. Though she has not revealed her cars and properties, she lives a luxurious and comfortable life with her baby boy.

Undoubtedly the richest star of Shahs of Sunset has multiple sources of income like casting in series, podcasts, and sponsors on her verified social media accounts.

Her Business: GG As An Entrepreneur

Golnesa Gharachedaghi owns several businesses. In 2018, she founded Wüsah, a medicinal Cannabis company in California, after the government of California legalized the recreational use of weed. 

The company sold products like pen vapes, pre-rollers, body balm, and many more. The main purpose of the company is to treat diseases like rheumatic arthritis without making the consumers high.

GG also co-founded G.G's Extensions with her sister Leila Gharachedaghi. Their business aims to provide easily attachable extensions like hair extensions. They have also patented new technology that is used to attach those extensions easily.

Often referred to as "The Queen of Cannabis" Golnesa has recently launched her new adult business, Intimately GG. The business is focused on selling sex toys for women. 

In an interview, she said she is doing this business to empower other women. She added:

"We deserve an intimacy for ourselves that doesn't require the presence of others."

Plastic Surgery: Did GG Go through Surgeries?

The TV model, as well as a businesswoman Golnesa, had also gone through plastic surgeries multiple times. She had done her nose job and filled her lips.

In 2015, GG had also done her breast enhancement and buttock injection as well.

GG's Struggle Against "RA" & Other Diseases

The co-founder of G.G's Extensions Golnesa has shared about the disease she has been struggling from over thirteen years. She has always been frank with her fans and shares about her fight against Rheumatoid Arthritis.  

Being a patient of an autoimmune disease like RA is not easy, especially when you are pregnant, she told her fans. To cope with the disease, she consumes cannabis. In 2015 she also started chemotherapy to treat Rheumatoid Arthritis. 

In one of The Daily Dish episodes, GG openly spoke about her hearing impairment and how she's adapted by reading lips. She has also told about the adjustments she has made in her life before and after using hearing aids.

I was born with a rare hearing loss and only have 28 [percent] hearing. And as I've gone through life missing out on many amazing sounds.

Bio & Wiki Of Golnesa

The Iranian-American entrepreneur Golnesa was born on November 27, 1981, in Los Angeles of, California, USA. She completed her high school graduation from a reputed high school in LA. She was totally into athletics and gymnastics on an Olympic level while she was in high school.

But later on after the completion of her high school, she decided not to continue her career as an athlete. 

Therefore she joined Loyola Marymount University In Los Angeles, California located on the west side of the city, near Playa Vista. She got her degree in Addiction treatment counseling.

Golnesa Has a Total of Eight Tattoos

GG often claims her as a huge tattoo fan, like Ruta Gedmintas. She has over eight tattoos in total on her body which she does not hesitate to show through her social media sites.

On top of that, she has tattoos on her wrist, feet, back, thighs, and inside lip.

Golnesaa: Alcoholism & Drugs Addiction 

Golnesa has been through a dark past full of drugs and other addictions. She revealed that she started doing drugs when she was eleven and a half. She has stated that she was completely addicted to cocaine and weed. 

Moreover, she claimed that the root cause of those addictions was going to parties, gatherings, and clubs with friends at an early age.

At the age of 23, Golnesa realized the negative effects of drugs and their impact on her life. Therefore she decided to admit into a rehabilitation center in 2004. At this time of date, she is free of drug abuse and other addictions. However, she still uses cannabis for medical purposes.

Despite being free from drugs for over 17 years, Golnesa still gets nightmares of going to that dark past again. In an interview with ET about her alcohol abuse at the show in 2015, she said:

"When the alcohol abuse picked up last year, I knew this pattern and I knew what was happening. I recognized it. I said, 'Okay, well if I don't stop it now, this could eventually lead to a few lines of coke, it could lead into a lot of other dangerous things."

Owner Of A Wonderful Pet 

Golnesa is currently staying with her son and her pet dog ZsaZsa. She owns a wonderful female Rottweiler of black-brown color.

Family: Parents & Siblings

GG comes from a wealthy family who shifted to California, USA, from Iran in 1975. She was raised by her mother and father. 

Her father, Mahmoud Gharachedaghi runs a boutique architecture firm whereas her mother works in LA textile and garment industry.

As mentioned earlier she also has a sister, Leila Gharachedaghi, with whom she co-founded G.G's Extensions.
