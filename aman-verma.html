Aman Yatan Verma is a well-known face of the Bollywood film industry and is known for his amazing acting skills. He has acted in various Indian movies for which he got huge recognition during his career life. While he is most popularly known for the role played in the debut movie “Sangharsh” which was released in the year 1999. Also, he has played a variety of roles for which he gained immense love and much appreciation from fans and viewers. Apart from the movie, Aman Verma has also performed in various TV serials for which he gained much popularity.

Career

In 1999, Aman Verma took the start of his career with a debut movie “Sangharsh” starring Akshay Kumar and Preity Zinta. Also, he made his debut TV serial “Pachpan Khambe Laal Deewarein” alongside Mita Vashisht in 1993. In 2002, Aman Verma appeared as a guest on the TV show named “Jaani Dushman”. In 2003, he worked in the movie “Koi Hai” in which he played a lead role as Raj Malhotra. In the same year, he also worked in some more movies including “Pran Jaye Par Shaan Na Jaye”, “Andaaz” starring Priyanka Chopra Jonas and Akshay Kumar, “Baaghban” starring Superstar Amitabh Bachchan”, Hema Malini, Salman Khan, Mahima Chaudhary and others who played roles effectively. In 2004, he also performed in “Tum-A Dangerous Obsession”  and as Hitesh in the movie “Home Delivery” which was released in the year 2005. In the same year, Aman Verma also appeared on the Magic Show “Vaah! Life Ho To Aisi” and “Dosti-Friends Forever”. In 2006, the most talented actor worked in “Kachchi Sadak”, “Love  Forever”, “Baabul” starring Salman Khan, Rani Mukherjee, and Amitabh Bachchan. While some of his hit movies include “Janani”, “Janeman”, “Superstar” and the list goes on. Likewise, he also worked in some TV shows and serials including most popular serials, like; Kyunki Saas Kabhi Bahu Thi”, “Kumkum”, “Viraasat”, “CID”, “Aik Larki Anjani Si” and the list goes on. However, he has done several tasks and performed very well.

Achievements

Among various notable achievements, Aman Verma was honored with the Indian Telly Awards for the category of Best Actor (Lead) for the serial “Kehta Hai Dil” in the year 2003. In 2015, he also honored with the Indian Television Academy Awards for the Best Actor category for the serial “Viraasat”.
