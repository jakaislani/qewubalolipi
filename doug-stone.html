Biography

Doug Stone was born in Toronto, Canada on December 27, 1950. Anime, animation, movie, and video game voice actor best known for his portrayal of Psycho Mantis in the Metal Gear Solid video game series. He also voiced several characters in M.A.S.K. and Dragonborg in Beetleborgs Metallix. He and Bryce Papenbrook both provided their voice for Lego Friends: The Power of Friendship.
